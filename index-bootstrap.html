About Me

Laura brings to this crazy-fast-paced-whirlwind of a boot camp a great sense of optimism, enthusiasm, and the understanding that this was NOT the month to give up caffeine.

Whether it's floating, div'ing, sectioning, justifying, id'ing, or classing, it's a guarantee that you'll find her in a six month Sublime fog, finding and losing the just right code over and over and over again.

What else piques her interest? Well, when she's not trying to "git" how to code, Laura travels the world in her homemade canoe, gives free hot air balloon rides to rescue pigs, arranges her many awards won from french braiding horse manes, and hand carves wooden shoes for children.

Want to know which of these things (if any or all) are true? Click on any of the "Contact Me" choices to the right!
